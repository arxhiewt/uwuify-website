Troubleshooting

Bot not responding?

Ensure the bot has the necessary permissions in your server.

Commands not appearing?

Make sure slash commands are synced. Re-invite with `applications.commands` permission if needed.

Message transformations delayed?

uwuify processes messages instantly, but network issues may cause minor delays.

Privacy Policy

What happens with your data?
uwuify processes messages sent by the user that is only being uwufied. uwuify never stores, shares, or sends your messages to any database. Your privacy is our priority, and live transformations are handled in real-time without logging.
