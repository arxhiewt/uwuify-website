troubleshooting

bot not responding?

check that uwuify has the correct permissions in your server.

commands not appearing?

make sure slash commands are synced. re-invite with applications.commands if needed.

messages delayed?

uwuify processes messages live, small network delays may happen.

privacy policy

what happens with your data?
uwuify processes messages sent by the user that is only uwufied. uwuify never stores, shares, or sends your messages to any database.
